    Reflection¶

    The goal of a reflection is to improve.

    One does so writing down how a passed day went: what did yo do, how did it go and how can it be improved?

    By forcing oneself to think of this, one can actually learn from the past.

    What did you do?¶

    i did a litterature study

    What did you do well?¶

    well i was very focused and didnt have any distractions

    What could be improved?¶

    i could be more on time and maybe remember what to bring what i need

    What did your supervisor do well?¶

    he asnwered my questions well and helped a lot.

    How can your supervisor be improved?¶

    i dont think there is anything to improve

    What would you do differently next time?¶

    be on time, have my materials.

    Which tip would you give to future PRAO students?¶

    Dont be shy to ask qusteions and dont have any distractions
